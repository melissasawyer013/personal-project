BLOCK COORDINATOR RESPONSIBILITIES

Block coordinators are individuals who have agreed to be a point-person on coordinating need fulfillment within the sub-neighborhood they are a part of. Duties include:

  • check the Needs & Offerings page regularly for needs and offerings on block

  • coordinate need fulfillment of neighbors in block

  • attend weekly DCCN check-ins via Zoom

  • reach out to other block coordinators and DCCN organizers when issues arise around need fulfillment and capacity
